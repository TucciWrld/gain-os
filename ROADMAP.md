## Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|
| Feature scope creep | High | Prioritize core features, defer nice-to-haves |
| Resource constraints | High | Focus on smaller team initially |
| Community contributions | Medium | Clear contributing guidelines |
| Compatibility issues | Medium | Extensive testing framework |
| Security vulnerabilities | High | Regular audits and patching |
